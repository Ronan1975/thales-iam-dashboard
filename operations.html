NIS2: Mandatory IAM Across 18 Sectors

NIS2 mandates access control, identity management, and MFA across all essential and important entities. 22 of 27 states have transposed. First audit deadline extended to June 30, 2026. Fines up to €10M or 2% global turnover. Management personally accountable. Source: ECSO Tracker; ENISA June 2025

DORA: 22,000 Financial Entities

DORA applies to ~22,000 financial entities. Requires real-time access monitoring, privileged session recording, JIT provisioning, incident reporting within 4 hours. Only 50% expected full compliance by end 2025. Fines up to 10% turnover. BFSI = 25.5% of EU IAM market. Sources: IBM; Epical; Ping Identity

EU AI Act: Identity for High-Risk AI

Full applicability August 2026. Biometric ID systems = high-risk. AI identity verification must meet both eIDAS and AI Act requirements. Audit trails must capture signing events AND AI decision logging. Fines up to 7% global revenue. Sources: EU Digital Strategy; DataGuard

eIDAS 2.0 & EU Digital Identity Wallet

Every EU member state must provide a certified EUDI Wallet by September 2026. 550 companies across 26 states are in large-scale pilots (POTENTIAL, NOBID, EWC, DC4EU). Use cases: eGov services, bank account opening, SIM registration, mobile driving licence, eSignature, ePrescription. 80% citizen digital ID adoption target by 2030. Thales is already eIDAS certified and handles 75% of Dutch eHerkenning traffic — this is a massive head start.

Auth for GenAI (GA April 30, 2026): Purpose-built identity for AI agents. Features: user authentication for agents, Token Vault (secure OAuth 2.0 connections to Gmail/Slack with auto-refresh), asynchronous authorization for long-running tasks, framework integrations (LangChain, LlamaIndex, Google GenKit, Vercel AI SDK), automated access reviews with human owner assignment. Sources: okta.com/newsroom; Okta Showcase 2026

Transmit Security (Forrester CIAM Leader): Unified fraud + IDV + auth in single platform. Real-time risk scoring via AI/ML across hundreds of telemetry points. Visual journey builder. Multi-session fraud detection. Sources: Forrester Wave CIAM Q4 2024; transmitsecurity.com

OneWelcome’s counter: None of these have (1) GDPR-native consent for AI interactions, (2) hardware-backed agent credentials via HSM, (3) European data sovereignty guarantees, or (4) eIDAS 2.0 wallet integration. Build the differentiation, don’t try to out-feature Auth0 on DX. Win on trust, consent, and compliance.
